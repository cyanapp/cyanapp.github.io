Contact

You can contact us through email, the following personal data may be entered optionally:

Name

E-mail address

These personal data are transmitted to us and stored.

The processing of the personal data from the e-mail is only for contacting and processing your request.

The legal basis for the processing of the data transmitted in the course of sending an e-mail is the legitimate interest in contacting for service reasons. If the establishment of contact aims at concluding a contract, the additional legal basis for the processing is the fulfilment of a contract.

The data will be deleted as soon as it is no longer necessary for the purpose of its collection. For the personal data sent by e-mail, this is the case when the respective conversation with the user has ended. The conversation has ended when it can be inferred from the circumstances that the relevant facts have been finally clarified.

The user has the possibility at any time to revoke his consent to the processing of personal data. For this you can either send us a renewed request for cancellation or declare your withdrawal by e-mail to your respective contact person. All personal data stored in the course of contacting will be deleted in this case.

Rights of the user

As a user of our mobile app, you have a right to free information about the stored personal data, their origin, their recipients and the purpose of the storage as well as a right to rectification, data portability, restriction of processing and erasure. Please note that we cannot erasure your data in any case, but for example only if the purpose of the data processing is omitted and there is no statutory storage requirement.

In order to exercise your rights, an informal notification to the above-mentioned data protection officer is sufficient. Please let us know which information or processing activity your request relates to, the format in which you wish to receive this information and whether the personal data should be sent to you or to another recipient.
